## 1. Engagement

1.1 Client engages Agency to identify and introduce candidates ("Candidates") for permanent positions with Client.

1.2 **Client engages Agency on an exclusive basis** for all roles within the Engineering and Product functions during the Term. Client is under no obligation to pay any additional fee for such exclusivity, and may also source candidates through its own efforts or other channels.

## 2. Fees

2.1 In consideration of a successful placement, Client shall pay Agency a placement fee equal to **eighteen percent (18%) of the Candidate's total first-year remuneration**, including base salary, guaranteed and discretionary bonuses, commissions, sign-on payments, equity, and any other compensation of any kind.

2.2 **The Fee shall be deemed earned only upon the Candidate's successful completion of a ninety (90) day probationary period** with the Client, and shall be invoiced thereafter.

2.3 Client shall pay all undisputed invoices **within sixty (60) days** of the invoice date.

## 3. Replacement Guarantee

3.1 If a placed Candidate ceases employment for any reason within **one hundred eighty (180) days** of their start date, Client shall be entitled, at Client's sole election, to **a full refund of the Fee paid**.

3.2 The refund under Section 3.1 shall apply **regardless of the reason for the Candidate's departure**, including where employment ends due to redundancy, restructuring, elimination of the role, or a change in the Client's business circumstances.

## 4. Introductions

4.1 An "introduction" occurs when Agency provides Client with a Candidate's name and CV.

4.2 Client agrees to consider Candidates introduced by Agency in good faith.

## 5. Liability and Indemnification

5.1 **Agency shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client from and against any and all losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses of any kind arising out of or relating to any Candidate introduced by Agency**, including losses arising from the Candidate's job performance, conduct, or departure after employment.

5.2 Client's liability under this Agreement shall in all cases be limited to the amount of Fees actually paid to Agency in the twelve (12) months preceding any claim.

## 6. Restrictive Covenants

6.1 For a period of **three (3) years** following the Effective Date, Agency shall not solicit, approach, or recruit **any employee of Client or any of Client's affiliates**, whether or not such employee was introduced or placed by Agency.

## 7. Term and Termination

7.1 This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months, and **shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month terms** unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal **no later than ninety (90) days prior** to the end of the then-current term.

7.2 Client may terminate this Agreement at any time on thirty (30) days' notice. Agency may terminate only for Client's material breach that remains uncured for sixty (60) days.

## 8. Assignment

8.1 **Client may assign this Agreement, in whole or in part, to any third party without Agency's consent.** Agency may not assign without Client's prior written consent.

## 9. Miscellaneous

9.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

9.2 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
