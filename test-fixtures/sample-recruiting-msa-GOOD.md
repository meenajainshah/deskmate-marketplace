# RECRUITMENT SERVICES AGREEMENT

**This Recruitment Services Agreement ("Agreement")** is entered into as of ____________, 2026 (the "Effective Date") by and between **Nimbus Talent Partners LLC** ("Agency") and **Vertex Dynamics, Inc.** ("Client").

## 1. Engagement

1.1 Client engages Agency on a **non-exclusive** basis to identify and introduce candidates ("Candidates") for permanent positions with Client. Client may engage other agencies or source directly.

## 2. Fees

2.1 For a successful placement, Client shall pay Agency a placement fee equal to **twenty percent (20%) of the Candidate's first-year annualised base salary**, excluding bonus, commission, equity, and sign-on payments unless expressly agreed in writing.

2.2 The Fee is **earned on the Candidate's start date** and payable within **thirty (30) days** of that date, independent of any probationary period.

2.3 Undisputed invoices unpaid after the due date accrue interest at **1.5% per month**.

## 3. Replacement Guarantee

3.1 If a placed Candidate leaves within **ninety (90) days** of their start date, Agency will provide **one replacement search at no additional fee**. If Agency cannot present a suitable replacement within thirty (30) days, Client is entitled to a **pro-rata refund** calculated as Fee × (days remaining in the guarantee period ÷ 90).

3.2 The guarantee does **not** apply where the Candidate's departure results from redundancy, restructuring, elimination of the role, a change in Client's circumstances, or Client's breach (including late payment of the Fee).

## 4. Candidate Ownership

4.1 An introduction occurs when Agency provides Client with a Candidate's name and CV.

4.2 Where Client, or any of its affiliates, parent, or subsidiaries, employs or engages an introduced Candidate **in any role within twelve (12) months** of introduction, the full Fee is payable.

4.3 **Client shall not circumvent Agency** by engaging an introduced Candidate directly or through any affiliate, parent, subsidiary, or third-party referral. This Section survives termination for twelve (12) months.

## 5. Liability and Indemnification

5.1 Each party shall indemnify the other for losses arising from its own fraud, gross negligence, or wilful misconduct. **Agency's indemnity does not extend to a Candidate's job performance or conduct once employed by Client.**

5.2 **Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement is capped at the Fees paid for the relevant placement**, except for liability arising from fraud or wilful misconduct.

## 6. Restrictive Covenants

6.1 For **twelve (12) months** following a placement, Agency shall not solicit **the specific Candidate it placed** with Client. This does not extend to Client's other employees, nor to candidates who approach Agency independently.

## 7. Term and Termination

7.1 This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for twelve (12) months. It renews only by written agreement of both parties.

7.2 Either party may terminate on thirty (30) days' written notice. Fee obligations for introductions made before termination survive under Section 4.

## 8. Assignment

8.1 Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's prior written consent, such consent not to be unreasonably withheld.

## 9. Miscellaneous

9.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

9.2 Each party shall handle candidate personal data in compliance with applicable law, including the CCPA/CPRA where relevant, and shall notify the other of any data breach affecting shared data.

9.3 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
