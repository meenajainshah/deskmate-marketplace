Privacy Policy

Deskmate plugin · Effective 10 July 2026
The short version: Deskmate has no servers, no backend, no accounts, and no telemetry. iView Labs does not collect, receive, store, or transmit your contracts, fees, candidate data, or any other information you use with the plugin. Everything runs inside your own Claude environment.

1. What Deskmate is

Deskmate is a plugin (a set of skills) that runs inside Anthropic's Claude products (Claude Code and Claude Cowork). It is published by iView Labs Pvt. Ltd. It reviews recruiting contracts, tracks placement fees, and builds role kits from documents and details you provide.

2. What we collect

Nothing. Deskmate has no server-side component operated by iView Labs. We do not run analytics, tracking, or telemetry, and we have no way to see the documents, terms, fees, candidate names, or prompts you use with the plugin.

3. Where your data lives

  • Documents you upload and the information you enter are processed in-session to produce the plugin's output.
  • The records Deskmate creates — the Deal Record (contract terms), placement ledger (fees), and Role Briefs — are stored in your own Claude workspace (local to you or your Claude account), not on any iView Labs system.
  • By default, Deskmate stores extracted terms only, not the full text of your contracts, unless you choose to keep the source text.

4. Processing by Anthropic

Because Deskmate runs on Claude, the content you send to it is processed by Anthropic's models to generate responses. That processing is governed by Anthropic's own terms and privacy policy (see anthropic.com/legal/privacy), not by iView Labs. Deskmate itself adds no additional collection, sharing, or storage.

5. Third parties and connectors

The current version of Deskmate works with zero connectors and shares your data with no third party. If you connect optional external tools in future versions (for example, an accounting or email tool), any data flow to those tools is initiated and authorized by you and governed by their privacy policies.

6. Sensitive information

Contracts, fee amounts, and candidate details are sensitive. Deskmate is designed to keep only the minimum it needs (extracted terms), to store it in your own workspace, and never to transmit it to iView Labs or to recipients you did not choose. Deskmate never sends emails, signs documents, or issues payments on its own — every such action requires your explicit approval.

7. Children

Deskmate is a business tool and is not directed to children under 16.

8. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
